Our Process

Our Brand Creation Workflow

01

Discovery Workshop & Audit

We research your business objectives, industry trends, and target personas to build a solid strategic foundation.

02

Strategy & Positioning Roadmap

We define your brand tone, messaging architecture, personality attributes, and competitive market positioning statements.

03

Logo Concepts & Moodboards

Our design team creates various initial logo concept options, color systems, and typographic pairings for review.

04

Fine-Tuning & Asset Finalization

We iterate on your selected direction, refining grid measurements, kerning details, and color specifications.

05

Collateral & Stationery Layouts

We expand the visual system across business cards, envelopes, presentation templates, packaging, and digital channels.

06

Brand Guidelines Delivery

We compile and package your complete brand handbook along with high-res source files in all vector and web formats.
